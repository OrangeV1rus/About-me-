I'm always eager to learn, whether through new experiences, meeting people with different perspectives, or reading books that inspire me. Currently, I’m diving into self-development books and philosophy to help shape my worldview. Outside of school and hobbies, I love spending time with friends and family. I’m also into photography, and you’ll often catch me snapping nature photos when I’m out on walks or hikes. That’s a bit about me. Thanks for visiting my page!
